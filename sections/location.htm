Conference Location

Delta Grand (Marriott) Burnaby Conference Centre is located at 4331 Dominion Street, Burnaby, BC (directions).

Inside the hotel, you will find a fitness room, sauna and hot tub, 24-hour business centre, and the Grand Villa Casino with live entertainment at Personas Lounge.

Parking is free, and there is a free shuttle to/from Gilmore Skytrain Station, Brentwood Town Centre, or Metrotown. Call 604-436-2211 for shuttle times.
